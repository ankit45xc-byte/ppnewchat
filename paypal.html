Troubleshooting Guides

Can’t Log In

Try resetting your password using the “Forgot Password” option. If you still can’t access, call support for verification.

Dispute a Payment

Open the Resolution Center in your account to file a dispute. Call support for guidance if needed.

Payment on Hold

Holds are common for new accounts. Confirm delivery or request release through support.

Security Awareness

Be cautious of phishing attempts. PayPal will never ask for your password or OTP over email. Always check the URL before logging in.
